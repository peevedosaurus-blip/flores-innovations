Our Services

Grain Bins

Grain Bin Services

Full-service grain bin installation and maintenance for farms of any scale. We travel to your site and get it done right, on schedule.

  • New bin installation & setup
  • Bin repair & structural work
  • Aeration & ventilation systems
  • Flooring & sweep auger install
  • Ladder & safety upgrades
  • On-site travel available nationwide
Millwright

Millwright Work

Precision millwright services for agricultural and industrial facilities. We install, align, and maintain the machinery your operation depends on.

  • Equipment installation & alignment
  • Conveyor & elevator systems
  • Bearing & shaft work
  • Preventive maintenance
  • Industrial troubleshooting
Fabrication

Custom Fabrication

One-off and production metal fab built to spec. If you can describe it, we can build it — from structural components to custom equipment mods.

  • Custom steel fabrication
  • Welding & structural repair
  • Equipment modifications
  • Guards, frames & supports
  • Shop & on-site work available
